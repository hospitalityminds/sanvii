About us

We Saanvi resort was established on 20th May, 2018 located at Koliyak near Bhavnagar, Gujarat. Saanvi resort is situated near Nishkalank Mahadev Temple which is one of the most visited places of pilgrimage in Bhavnagar and also just a few kilometers away from our resort. Saanvi resort is much more than a resort. It represents a new wave of hospitality with the promise of luxury services and facilities at affordable prices. The resort has been designed with a lively ambiance, a beautiful garden, ample space, and abundant natural light.

Luxury Resort

The space at Saanvi resort captures warmth, friendly vibes which makes the experience more enjoyable while relaxing. The resort offers luxuriously appointed deluxe and super deluxe rooms which are air-conditioned, spacious, contain electric tea pots, desk, free WIFI, room service and certain rooms of our resort also have a majestic garden view.
