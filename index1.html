Welcome to Saanvi Resort

Saanvi Resort is one of the best resorts in Bhavnagar, located at beachfront Koliyaak near well-known Nishkalank Mahadev Temple, Gujarat. This resort has rooms with light-painted walls and dark brown wood furnishings with the finest amenities for your comfort and leisure. The resort offers a vegetarian multi-cuisine restaurant, party plot, a banquet hall is available for our guests to organize any events, free Wi-Fi, power backup, CCTV cameras, garden & parking facilities and playground.
